We automatically collect certain information when you access the API for security and performance purposes, including:

  • IP Address: To prevent abuse, rate-limit requests, and protect against attacks.
  • Device Info: Device model and OS version to optimize the user experience.

Usage & Analytics Data (Google Firebase Analytics):

To improve our service, we may use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us understand how the App is used (e.g., popular features). This includes:

  • Interaction Data: Screens visited, buttons clicked, and successful actions (like "Plan Generated").
  • Plan Preferences: Aggregated data on diet types, goals, and activity levels. No personal identifiers are linked to this data.

Consent & Opt-Out: You can enable or disable this data collection at any time. By default, this collection is DISABLED until you click "Allow".

Opt-Out: If you wish to opt-out from receiving these types of communications, you may turn them off in your device's settings or the Application's profile settings.

7. Data Deletion & Your Rights

In compliance with the DPDP Act 2023 and Google Play User Data policies, you have the right to request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data.

How to delete your data:

Process:
Once we receive your request, we will send a confirmation email to verify your identity. Upon confirmation, your data will be permanently purged from our servers within 7 days. This action is irreversible.
